## 🎓 Our Product — Edvronix

> **School Management System built on Frappe/ERPNext**

Edvronix is our flagship product — a complete school management system designed specifically for private schools in Pakistan and the GCC. Currently live at schools managing **1,500+ students**.

### Modules
- 📋 Student Admissions & Enrollment
- 💰 Fee Management & Parent-wise Vouchers
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Parent Portal — fees, attendance, results
- 👨‍💼 Employee Portal — HR, attendance, payroll
- 📦 Supplier & Inventory Management (books, uniforms, stationery)
- 📱 WhatsApp Fee Reminders to Parents
- 📊 Reports & Analytics
- 🏫 Timetable & Scheduling

### Why Edvronix
- Built on **ERPNext** — accounting, HR, and school management in one system
- **No separate sync** — fees flow directly into accounts receivable and P&L
- **Local WhatsApp support** — from the team that built it
- **Live in 3 weeks** — guaranteed
